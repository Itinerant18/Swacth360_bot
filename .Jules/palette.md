## 2024-04-11 - Icon-only buttons accessibility\n**Learning:** In Dexter-bot, numerous interactive icon-only buttons (like those for regenerating, copying, feedback, and editing in chat) rely solely on the `title` attribute for tooltips. For screen readers, `aria-label` is required to ensure these critical actions are announced.\n**Action:** Audit and update `src/components/Chat/MessageBubble.tsx` and `src/components/Chat/ConversationSidebar.tsx` to include `aria-label` alongside `title` on icon-only interactive elements.
