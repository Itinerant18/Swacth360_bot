## Hestia Pro Fire Alarm - Diagnostic Shortcuts & View Modes

This details the specific keypad shortcuts used by installers to simulate hardware faults on the Hestia Pro, alongside the configurable user interface view modes [6].

```text
=== ZONE FAULT TESTING SHORTCUTS ===
To test the physical loop integrity without requiring actual hardware faults:

[ SHORT CIRCUIT TEST ]
Press Key <1> + Press [ISOLATE] simultaneously  ---> Simulates a Zone Short [7]

[ OPEN CIRCUIT TEST ]
Press Key <4> + Press [ISOLATE] simultaneously  ---> Simulates a Zone Open [7]


=== VIEW MODE CONFIGURATION ===
Allows the LCD to display zones geographically rather than numerically [8].

[ Standard View ]           [ Floor View Mode ]
 ZONE 1              --->    GROUND FLOOR [8]
 ZONE 2              --->    FLOOR 1 [8]
 ZONE 3              --->    FLOOR 2 [8]
 ZONE 4              --->    FLOOR 3 [8]
